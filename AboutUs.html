About Jaded Jewlers

I started Jaded Jewelers because I am a mom of three beautiful kids and I wanted to create a job for myself that would allow me to do something I love and also grant me more time with them at home. I thought, why not turn my hobby into an income. So, I did. Since then I have managed to turn this into my dream company Jaded Jewelers. We take our jewelry making very seriously and only provide you with the best products. We strive to always have the best quality jewelry; pieces you will be able to enjoy for years to come. Our main motivator for ideas is to make things that we would love to wear ourselves but can’t find in other stores. Our signature style is minimalistic and we aspire to continue creating unique pieces for all of our amazing customers. Our goal is to provide you with beautiful, unique and handmade pieces. All of our pieces are handmade by our creators and each are unique and unlike any other. I have drawn out each and every design and have many customizable pieces available. The pieces we create for you are much more than just jewelry, they are an experience. An experience that you get to have with a loved one upon gifting them one of our pieces, or even a gift for yourself, because let’s be real you deserve to look fabulous.
